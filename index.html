MEET MARK HEINONEN

A New Perspective for Houghton County

Mark Heinonen isn't a career politician. He is a licensed Realtor with the 906 Real Estate Group, working every day to help families find their homes and businesses find their foundations in the Upper Peninsula.

"I believe in an honest, optimistic view of our area. No sales-speak, just real talk about where our community is going."

From the Keweenaw down to Gogebic, Mark understands the unique challenges of the Western UP. He brings a professional background in negotiating complex deals, understanding property rights, and fostering economic growth without sacrificing the character of our towns.

The Platform

Priorities for District 5

Smart Housing & Zoning

As a Realtor, I see the housing shortage firsthand. We need sensible zoning that encourages development of workforce housing while protecting the natural beauty that makes the Copper Country unique.

Transparent Governance

My business philosophy is "No Sales-Speak." I will bring that same clarity to the County Commission. No hidden agendas, just honest conversations about budgets, taxes, and services.

Economic Connectivity

District 5 deserves a voice that fights for infrastructure. From reliable internet for remote workers to road maintenance that survives our winters, we need to connect our local businesses to the world.

Serving The Community

District 5 is the heart of the Copper Country. It requires a commissioner who understands land value, property rights, and the importance of tourism and local industry.

  • Protecting Property Rights
  • Supporting Small Business Owners
  • Honest Budget Allocation
  • Preserving Recreational Access
